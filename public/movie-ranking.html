Must Watch Movies According to Martin

Movies with a great story

The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption is an inspiring story of hope, friendship, and resilience within the harsh confines of a prison.

The Green Mile

The Green Mile is a powerful and emotional tale of compassion and injustice set against the backdrop of a death row prison.

Dances with Wolves

Dances with Wolves is beautiful because of its captivating story of cultural encounter and personal transformation.
